Kakashi Hatake is a fictional character from the Naruto manga and anime series. He is known for his mysterious personality and exceptional ninja skills.

Eren Yeager is the protagonist of the Attack on Titan manga and anime series. He is determined to fight against the Titans and protect humanity.

Levi Ackerman, also known as Captain Levi, is a prominent character in the Attack on Titan series. He is a skilled soldier and the leader of the Special Operations Squad.

At the start of the series, it is revealed that Naruto was born as the vessel for the Nine-Tailed Fox, a malevolent and powerful creature that attacked the Hidden Leaf Village. In order to protect the village, the Fourth Hokage, Naruto's own father, sacrificed his life to seal the Nine-Tailed Fox inside Naruto. As a result, Naruto grew up without knowing his parents and faced the resentment and fear of the villagers who saw him as the embodiment of the monster that had ravaged their home.
